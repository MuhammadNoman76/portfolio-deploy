About Me

As a final year Computer Science student at Iqra University, I specialize in bridging the gap between Natural Language Processing and Urdu language technology. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions to preserve and enhance Urdu's digital presence.

I've created UrduLughaat, a comprehensive preprocessing library for Urdu text, and implemented various NLP models including BERT-based chatbots and sentiment analysis systems. My technical stack includes Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and modern web technologies.

When I'm not coding, I contribute to open-source projects and mentor junior developers in NLP fundamentals. My goal is to make Urdu a first-class citizen in the world of natural language processing.
